Algorithim for multiplication table

step1:start 
step2:declare variables i,n,res
step3:read values into i,n (n=1,i=2)
step4:check n<=10 if yes go to step5 otherwise go to step 9
step5:calculate i*n =res
step6:print res 
step7:increment the n value by 1
step8:repeat step4,5,6,7 until n<=10
step9:stop
